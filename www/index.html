🍽 Restaurant script — step by step
🗺
From door to dessert
A complete script for any sit-down restaurant in Japan
1
Entering
Say Konnichiwa and smile. Wait to be seated — don't sit down before staff guides you. Follow them to your table.
2
Ordering
Point at the menu item and say Kore onegaishimasu. Repeat for each item. For quantity, hold up fingers.
For multiple items: point and say "kore" each time, then "onegaishimasu" at the end.
3
Drinks
Say Mizu onegaishimasu for water. For other drinks, point at the menu.
4
After eating
Compliment the chef: Oishii! When leaving, say Arigatou gozaimasu to the staff. It's always warmly received.
5
Paying
Say O-kanjo onegaishimasu for the bill. Payment is usually at a register near the door, not at the table.
Many casual spots use ticket machines (pay first!) or have picture menus — just point and the system works perfectly without any Japanese.
